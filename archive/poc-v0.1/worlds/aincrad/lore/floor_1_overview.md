# Floor 1 — The Beginning Plains

Freeform lore the narrator may draw on for tone and texture. Nothing in this
file is state; the simulation never reads it.

Floor 1 is ten kilometers of grassland, low forest, and farmland ringing the
Town of Beginnings, the largest city in Aincrad and the place every trapped
player woke on day zero. The Monument of Life in the Black Iron Palace lists
every player's name — and strikes them through as they die.

West of the city, boar-trampled fields roll toward Horunka, a village of a
few dozen households under the eaves of the deep forest, where the trees close
in and carnivorous plants hunt by scent. North, past windmills, Tolbana sits
in view of the labyrinth's red tower.

The mood of the floor is held breath: thousands still refuse to leave the
starting city, guilds argue over who may spend lives on the labyrinth, and
bread gets a little more expensive every week the front doesn't move.
