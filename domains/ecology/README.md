# domains/ecology/

**Charter:** Vol. III Ch. 10 — Ecology. Every count has a cause. Nothing spawns;
everything descends (the forest does not respawn, Ch. 10 Designer Note).

**Owns:** populations (aggregate counts — the single truth living stocks reduce to,
Ruling 1); food webs; carrying capacity (derived from habitat, never authored);
population dynamics; habitats; migration; succession; the individuation seam
(Ruling 12).

**Never owns:** individual organisms while instantiated (Living Systems), the
environment itself (Physical), extraction economics (Resources/Economy), human
decisions, weather and climate (Physical) (§10.3).

**Key contracts:** no population changes count without an accounted cause; aggregate
and individual representations reconcile exactly across the seam; extinct locally means
absent until migration or reintroduction; ecological time runs whether or not any
observer attends — no frozen duchies (§10.8; Vol. V Ch. 5 §5.2). No spawn tables (§10.9).

**Appendix A:** Ruling 1 (one truth for living stocks), Ruling 12 (the seam balances).
