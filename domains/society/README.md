# domains/society/

**Charter:** Vol. III Ch. 5 — Society. Relationships that outlive the moments that
created them. The village remembers (Ch. 5, Designer Note).

**Owns:** kinship and lineage (append-only history); households; settlements (identity
independent of members and masonry — Ruling 5); roles; membership; social standing;
reputation — the per-community aggregate of witnessed and transmitted deeds.

**Never owns:** the persons themselves (organisms + information + holdings), formal
power (Institutions), shared meaning (Culture), individual opinion (information layer —
Ruling 2) (§5.3).

**Key contracts:** reputation is local to a community, never global, and changes only
through information that actually reached it; kinship history is append-only; no global
faction meter (§5.8, §5.9).

**Appendix A:** Ruling 2 (aggregate vs. opinion), Ruling 5 (community vs. masonry).
