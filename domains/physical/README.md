# domains/physical/

**Charter:** Vol. III Ch. 1 — Physical Reality. The stage every other domain acts on.
The only mandatory domain: every world has a *where* (Vol. IV Ch. 2 §2.1).

**Owns:** position, orientation, adjacency; topology and connectivity; regions and
containment; material composition and properties; environmental state (temperature,
water, light, weather); physical constraints (blocking, visibility, passability).

**Never owns:** biology, decisions, ownership, economics, governments, culture,
rendering (Ch. 1 §1.3). Physics is a consumer, not the center (Ch. 1, Designer Note).

**Key contracts:** single source of physical truth; representation independence
(consumers ask questions, never see grids/graphs/voxels); stable identity for places
(Ch. 1 §1.14). Read-mostly: the world must be inexpensive to observe (§1.12).

**Appendix A:** Ruling 5 (the settlement is Society's; the masonry is ours),
Ruling 9 (Conflict proposes structural damage; our rules apply it),
Ruling 11 (the record's medium is ours; its content is Knowledge's).
