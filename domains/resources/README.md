# domains/resources/

**Charter:** Vol. III Ch. 3 — Resources. Makes scarcity a fact: identity, quantity,
and renewal for usable matter. The bridge from physical substance to economic value.

**Owns:** resource definitions; deposits and grades; stock quantities (non-living);
regeneration and depletion rules; extraction yield functions; true discovery state.

**Never owns:** value or price (Economy), ownership (Society/Institutions), knowledge
of deposits (information layer), manufactured goods, the matter itself (Physical) (§3.3).
Living stocks are **Ecology's count; we expose the extractable view** — Ruling 1, the
no-twin-ledgers rule.

**Key contracts:** stocks are finite and authoritative; extraction always debits;
nonrenewable means nonrenewable; collapse thresholds, once crossed, hold; depletion is
history and never silently resets (§3.8). No infinite nodes, no spawning supply (§3.9).

**Appendix A:** Ruling 1 (living stocks: one truth, Ecology owns, we view).
